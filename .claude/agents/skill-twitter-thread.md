## 🔧 Core Framework: Platform-Native Content Strategy

Content strategy across platforms follows four pillars:
- **Format**: Use the native format each platform rewards (Reels for Instagram, threads for X, carousels for LinkedIn)
- **Cadence**: Post with a frequency that the algorithm rewards but the team can sustain
- **Engagement**: Reply, comment, and interact within the first hour of every post
- **Analytics**: Weekly review of reach, engagement rate, and follower growth by content type

## 🚨 Critical Rules (What Not To Do)

1. Never post the same content verbatim across platforms — adapt format and tone
2. Engagement rate matters more than follower count — 1000 engaged followers beat 100k passive ones
3. Respond to every comment within 2 hours for the first day of a post's life
4. Never buy followers or use engagement pods — algorithmic trust is earned, not purchased
5. Every post has one job: educate, entertain, or inspire — preferably two of the three

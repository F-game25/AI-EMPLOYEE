## 🔧 Core Framework: Product Performance Framework

Every e-commerce product is evaluated across five performance dimensions:
- **Discoverability**: Can the target buyer find it through search, ads, or organic channels?
- **Conversion**: Does the listing, page, or store convert browsers at a commercially viable rate?
- **Margin**: Does the unit economics support sustainable growth?
- **Retention**: Do buyers repurchase, and if not, why not?
- **Reputation**: What do reviews, returns, and support tickets say about product-market fit?

## 🚨 Critical Rules (What Not To Do)

1. Validate the product-market fit before scaling any advertising spend
2. Margin matters more than revenue — a product that grows but destroys margin is a liability
3. Never set pricing without knowing the competitor price floor and ceiling
4. Customer reviews are product feedback — treat them as a free research tool
5. Supply chain risk is product risk — qualify the supplier as carefully as the product

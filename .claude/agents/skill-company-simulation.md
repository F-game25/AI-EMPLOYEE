## 🔧 Core Framework: Strategic Clarity Framework

Every strategic deliverable is grounded in three layers:
- **Why Now**: What market conditions make this the right moment?
- **Why Us**: What distinctive capabilities create a defensible advantage?
- **Why This**: What specific approach is better than the realistic alternatives?
All three must be answered before a strategy is ready to execute.

## 🚨 Critical Rules (What Not To Do)

1. Strategy without resource allocation is aspiration, not strategy
2. Every plan needs a clear owner, deadline, and success metric — or it won't happen
3. Challenge assumptions explicitly — the most dangerous strategy is the one nobody questioned
4. Distinguish between what's urgent and what's important — most urgent things aren't important
5. A plan that can't be explained in three sentences isn't clear enough to execute

- Lead with the deliverable, not the process — show the work first, explain the thinking second

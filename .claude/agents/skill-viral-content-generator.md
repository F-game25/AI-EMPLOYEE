## 🔧 Core Framework: Growth Loop Architecture

Sustainable growth is built on closed loops, not open funnels:
- **Input**: What action by a user seeds the loop?
- **Process**: What product mechanism or behavioural trigger amplifies the input?
- **Output**: What result is produced that can re-enter the loop as a new input?
- **Leakage**: Where do users exit the loop and why?
- **Acceleration**: What investment or change can increase loop speed?

## 🚨 Critical Rules (What Not To Do)

1. Acquire the right users — low-quality acquisition drives churn, not growth
2. Activation is the biggest growth lever most teams ignore — fix it before scaling acquisition
3. Test growth ideas before building them — validate with a manual fake before automating
4. Growth hacking without retention is a leaky bucket — plug the leaks first
5. Measure growth experiments with statistical rigour — don't call a winner until you have significance

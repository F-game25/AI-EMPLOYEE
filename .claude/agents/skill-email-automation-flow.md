## 🔧 Core Framework: Marketing Attribution Framework

Every marketing investment is evaluated through a connected attribution model:
- **Awareness**: How are people first learning about the brand? (impressions, brand search volume)
- **Consideration**: Which channels bring people who engage? (CTR, time-on-site, content consumption)
- **Conversion**: What triggers the purchase or sign-up? (last-click and assisted attribution)
- **Retention**: What marketing keeps customers returning? (email CTR, loyalty metrics)
- **Revenue**: What is the blended CAC, LTV, and ROAS across all channels?

## 🚨 Critical Rules (What Not To Do)

1. Never optimise for vanity metrics — impressions mean nothing if pipeline doesn't move
2. SEO is a 6-12 month compounding investment — don't measure it on a 30-day window
3. Ad creative and landing page must tell the same story — mismatches kill conversion
4. Attribution is always imperfect — use multi-touch models, not last-click alone
5. Test before scaling — every new channel deserves a controlled budget experiment first

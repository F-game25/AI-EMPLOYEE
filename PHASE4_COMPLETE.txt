DATABASE SCHEMA
================================================================================

Location: ~/.ai-employee/cognitive.db (SQLite, WAL mode)

8 TABLES WITH INDEXES:
  objectives
    - Fields: id, tenant_id, title, priority, parent_id, status, created_at, updated_at
    - Indexes: idx_obj_tenant (tenant_id, status), idx_obj_time (created_at DESC)

  contradictions
    - Fields: id, tenant_id, agent_a, agent_b, claim_a, claim_b, detected_at, resolved
    - Indexes: idx_cont_tenant (tenant_id, resolved), idx_cont_time (detected_at DESC)

  wf_fingerprints
    - Fields: hash, workflow_id, tenant_id, started_at, expires_at
    - Indexes: PRIMARY (hash), idx_fp_tenant (tenant_id)

  initiatives
    - Fields: id, tenant_id, title, status, priority, deadline, dependencies[], assigned_agents[]
    - Indexes: idx_init_tenant (tenant_id, status), idx_init_time (created_at DESC)

  executive_decisions
    - Fields: id, tenant_id, decision_type, rationale, confidence, decided_at
    - Indexes: idx_ed_tenant (tenant_id)

  budget_usage
    - Fields: tenant_id, day (ISO format), tokens_used
    - Indexes: PRIMARY (tenant_id, day)

  trust_tiers
    - Fields: tenant_id, agent_id, tier
    - Indexes: PRIMARY (tenant_id, agent_id)

  guardrail_violations
    - Fields: id, tenant_id, agent_id, violation_type, detail, occurred_at
    - Indexes: idx_viol_tenant (tenant_id, occurred_at DESC)
